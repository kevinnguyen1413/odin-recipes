Flan

Description

This is a recipe to make a Latin dessert. The famous flan that everybody knows and loves.

This recipe is a classic, and it would be the perfect addition to close up a family party.

Ingredients

Caramel

  • 90g sugar
  • 15mL water

Pudding

  • 500mL milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 45g sugar
  • 250mL heavy cream
  • 1-1.5tbsp vanilla extract

Use hot water bath

Bake at 130 Celsius for 50 minutes

Do not rush! We do not want to overcook the eggs inside. Slow and steady will give the best result

Refrigerate for at least 4 hours
